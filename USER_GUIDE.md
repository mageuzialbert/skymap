## Registration Requirements

### Before You Register

To complete your business registration, you will need to provide:

#### 1. Sender ID Suggestion

You must suggest a **Sender ID** for SMS notifications. This is the name that will appear when customers receive SMS updates about their deliveries.

**Requirements:**
- Must be 11 characters or less
- Alphanumeric characters only
- Should represent your business name or brand
- Must be approved by your SMS provider

**Example Sender IDs:**
- `KASICOURIER`
- `YOURBIZ`
- `DELIVERY01`

#### 2. Electronic Stamp and Sign

You need to provide an **electronic stamp and signature** for official documents and invoices. This will be used for:
- Invoice generation
- Official communications
- Document authentication

**Format Requirements:**
- Digital image file (PNG, JPG, or SVG)
- Clear and professional appearance
- Recommended size: 200x200 pixels minimum

#### 3. SMS Cost Information

**SMS Notification Cost:** TZS 25 per SMS

SMS notifications are automatically sent for:
- Delivery status updates
- Rider assignment notifications
- Delivery completion confirmations
- Failed delivery alerts

The cost is included in your weekly invoice.

### Delivery Status Lifecycle

Understanding the delivery process:

```
CREATED → ASSIGNED → PICKED_UP → IN_TRANSIT → DELIVERED
                                         ↘ FAILED
```

**Status Descriptions:**

1. **CREATED**
   - Your delivery request has been submitted
   - Waiting for staff to assign a rider

2. **ASSIGNED**
   - A rider has been assigned to your delivery
   - Rider receives notification via SMS

3. **PICKED_UP**
   - Rider has collected the package
   - Drop-off customer receives SMS notification

4. **IN_TRANSIT**
   - Package is on the way to destination
   - Real-time tracking available

5. **DELIVERED**
   - Package successfully delivered
   - Both business and customer receive confirmation SMS
   - Delivery included in weekly invoice

6. **FAILED**
   - Delivery attempt unsuccessful
   - Business receives notification
   - Can be retried or cancelled

### SMS Notifications

Automatic SMS notifications are sent for:

- **Rider Assignment:** When a rider is assigned to your delivery
- **Pickup Confirmation:** When package is picked up (sent to drop-off customer)
- **Delivery Completion:** When package is delivered (sent to business and customer)
- **Failed Delivery:** When delivery attempt fails (sent to business)

**SMS Cost:** TZS 25 per SMS (included in weekly invoice)

## How-To Guides

### How to Register a New Business Account

1. **Navigate to Registration**
   - Go to https://kasicourier.ipab.co.tz/register
   - Click **"Register"** button

2. **Fill Registration Form**
   - Enter your **Business Name**
   - Enter your **Phone Number** (this will be your username)
   - Create a **Password** (minimum 8 characters)
   - Select your **District** (optional)

3. **Verify Phone Number**
   - Click **"Send Verification Code"**
   - Enter the 6-digit code received via SMS
   - Click **"Verify"**

4. **Complete Profile**
   - After login, go to **Profile Settings**
   - Add your business address
   - Upload business logo (optional)
   - Verify your email address

5. **Submit Required Documents**
   - Contact support to submit:
     - Sender ID suggestion
     - Electronic stamp and signature

6. **Start Using the Platform**
   - Once approved, you can start creating deliveries!

### How to Create a Delivery Request

1. **Access Deliveries Page**
   - Login to your business dashboard
   - Click **"Deliveries"** in the navigation menu
   - Or click **"Create New Delivery"** from the dashboard

2. **Fill Delivery Form**
   - **Pickup Information:**
     - Enter pickup address
     - Enter pickup contact name
     - Enter pickup phone number
   - **Drop-off Information:**
     - Enter drop-off address
     - Enter drop-off contact name
     - Enter drop-off phone number
   - **Package Details:**
     - Enter package description (optional)

3. **Submit Delivery**
   - Click **"Create Delivery"** button
   - Your delivery request is now submitted
   - Status will be **CREATED**

4. **Track Your Delivery**
   - View delivery in your deliveries list
   - Status will update automatically
   - You'll receive SMS notifications at each stage

### How to Track a Delivery

1. **View Deliveries List**
   - Go to **Dashboard → Deliveries**
   - All your deliveries are listed in a table

2. **Filter by Status**
   - Use the status filter dropdown
   - Select status: All, Created, Assigned, Picked Up, In Transit, Delivered, or Failed
   - View filtered results

3. **View Delivery Details**
   - Click on any delivery to view details
   - See complete information:
     - Pickup and drop-off addresses
     - Contact information
     - Package description
     - Current status
     - Delivery timeline
     - Assigned rider (if assigned)

4. **Monitor Status Updates**
   - Status updates automatically
   - Receive SMS notifications
   - View status history in delivery details

### How to View and Download Invoices

1. **Access Invoices Page**
   - Login to your business dashboard
   - Click **"Invoices"** in the navigation menu

2. **View Invoice List**
   - See all your invoices in a table
   - Information displayed:
     - Invoice number
     - Period (week start - week end)
     - Total amount
     - Status
     - Generation date

3. **View Invoice Details**
   - Click on any invoice to view full details
   - See complete breakdown:
     - Company information
     - Business information
     - Invoice items (deliveries)
     - Payment instructions
     - Total amount

4. **Print/Download Invoice**
   - Click **"Print / PDF"** button in invoice details
   - Invoice opens in print-friendly format
   - Use browser print function to save as PDF

5. **Payment Instructions**
   - Payment instructions are included in each invoice
   - Follow the instructions to make payment
   - Contact support if you have questions

### How to Update Business Profile

1. **Access Profile Settings**
   - Login to your business dashboard
   - Click **"Profile"** in the navigation menu

2. **Update Business Information**
   - **Business Logo:**
     - Click **"Upload Logo"** to add/change logo
     - Supported formats: PNG, JPG, SVG, WebP
     - Maximum size: 5MB
   - **Business Name:**
     - Update your business name
   - **Address Information:**
     - Update street address
     - Update city
     - Update postal code
     - Select region and district

3. **Change Password**
   - Scroll to **"Password Settings"** section
   - Enter new password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Confirm new password
   - Click **"Update Password"**

4. **Save Changes**
   - Click **"Save Changes"** button
   - Changes are saved immediately

**Note:** Phone number and email cannot be changed from profile page. Verify them in the Verification page.

### How to Verify Phone and Email

1. **Access Verification Page**
   - Login to your business dashboard
   - Click **"Verify"** in the navigation menu

2. **Verify Phone Number**
   - Your phone number is displayed
   - Click **"Send Verification Code"**
   - Enter the 6-digit code received via SMS
   - Click **"Verify Phone"**
   - Status will update to **Verified**

3. **Verify Email Address**
   - Your email address is displayed
   - Click **"Send Verification Email"**
   - Check your email inbox
   - Click the verification link in the email
   - Status will update to **Verified**

4. **Check Verification Status**
   - Green checkmark indicates verified
   - Red X indicates not verified
   - Verification is required for account security

1. **Access Quick Order**

5. **Fill Delivery Details**

     - Pickup address
